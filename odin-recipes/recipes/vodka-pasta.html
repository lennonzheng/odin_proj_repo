Penne alla Vodka

Description:

I found this dish on Epicurious. I found the description on the website is a bit vague, so I have added more details :) It is a simple, flavorful, around 30min dish.

Ingredients:

  • Salt
  • 1 35-ounce can Italian chopped tomatoes (better with San Marzano brand)
  • 1 pound penne
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp unsalted buttler or olive oil for finishing sauce
  • 2 to 3 tbsp chopped fresh Italian parsley
  • Some freshley grated Parmigiano-Reggiano, or mazzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Bring salted water to boil
  2. At the same time, in a saucepan, add olive oil with medium heat.
  3. Once olive oil is hot enought, add garlic. Stir until garlic are lightly browned.
  4. Slowly pour in a can of chopped tomatoes, be careful it will splash.
  5. When the water is boiling, add pasta.
  6. When the sauce is boiling, season with salt and pepper, as well as red chilli pepper.
  7. Pour in the vodka, stir for 2min. Then reduce heat to low, cover until pasta is ready.
  8. Pour in cream milk, give it a stir, and add in butter or olive oil.
  9. Pour in the penne, give it a good stir to make sure penne are coated with the sauce.
  10. Check seasoning, add salt and red pepper if necessary.
  11. When plating, sprinkle with cheese and parsley.
